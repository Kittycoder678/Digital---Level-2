About the site

Welcome to Minecraft Marketplace Tutorials! In this website you'll find a variety of tutorials on how to complete various worlds on the Bedrock Minecraft Marketplace.

Totems

Totems is a world by The Misfit Society, creators of Spring-a-Majig. In this world you explore multiple different areas to find the materials to construct totems. The aim of the game is to succeed in building all the totems. This website has guides to finding all the different blocks in the different areas, to assist you in creating the totems

Simburbia

Simburbia is a city simulator world by Jigarbov Productions. In this world, you are put in the position of mayor of a town, and it's your job to build up the town, by zoning various constructions, from power plants, to residential areas. The game doesn't have an end, however there are many projects for you to complete, and achievements for you to collect, which this website has guides to assist you with.
